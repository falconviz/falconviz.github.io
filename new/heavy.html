Heavy lifting

Home  /  Drones  /  Heavy lifting
The FalconViz FX4 Security & Emergency Response drone delivers a new precedent for autonomous surveillance. The FX4 is optimized for agility, endurance and ruggedness to handle the toughest environments. The high powered and efficient propulsion system allows for 15m/s flights covering a 1-2km radius. The automatic take-off and landing system allows for the FX4 to be launched from a command center and arrive at its point of interest within minutes.

The ground breaking propulsion system allows for 30 minute flight times which is 2X the average flight time of most drones. The FX4 payload allows both day and night surveillance with both a 8K Day camera (42MP) and Flir Thermal IR night camera. The sturdy and efficient gimbal system allows for smooth video and image capture even in windy conditions.

The FX4 is directly integrated with the FalconViz Ground Control Station that can be installed within any operating system so that responses can be coordinated between the security team and the drone. The FX4 can be automatically dispatched to exact GPS locations of any security emergency or threat within a 1-2km radius. Once the FX4 arrives on the scene it can stream back live video to the command center during day or night.
