Disaster Relife

Home  /  Industries  /  Disaster Relife
When disaster strikes, it is sometimes extremely difficult to asses the gravity of the situation. Our UAVs can be easily and quickly deployed in order to have an eye in the sky. With live video downstream and long autonomy, FalconViz drones can aid any relief efforts by providing instant situational awareness.
