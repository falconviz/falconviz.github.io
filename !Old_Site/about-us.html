THE
FALCON VIZ
ADVANTAGE

Using our unique multi-rotor copter
designs and integrated software, our
solution has several advantages >

  • The minicopter’s low altitude enables scanning of super high resolution data in diverse environments.
  • 3D scans are processed in real-time allowing onsite inspection and assessment.
  • Automated mission plans can be repeated monthly or even hourly. Buildings can be scanned multiple times to document changes.
  • Our software enables us to see immediate scanned results while still in the field and calculate new flight paths to insure total capture of desired areas.
  • Our technology is the only current fully integrated solution for generating complete multi-angle 3D models. FalconViz captures from multiple angles allowing creation of complete 3D models not just roofs or facades.
  • Our scanning solution is robust enough to handle pedestrians and vehicles moving through the scanned area.
  • Our scanning is safe, non-obtrusive and does not use dangerous lasers.
  • Our approach has a lower operating cost in comparison to traditional 3D surveying techniques which means we can offer our service at competitive prices without sacrificing quality.

About

Falcon Viz is a 3D aerial scanning and modeling system utilizing multi-rotor copters with AerialSfM, a proprietary 3D modelling software. Falcon Viz provides a unique and highly effective solution for cross industry and research requirements.
